Topic 2: The Dominance of Google Chrome

Exploring the rise and impact of Chrome in the browser market.

Chrome Market Share

In terms of market share in 2025, Google Chrome is currently the most dominant browser worldwide with more than half of global usage at 69.23%. Other popular browsers is Safari, which is the default browser on iPhones and Macs at 14.98%, Microsoft Edge, which replaced Internet Explorer in 2015 at 5.03%, and Mozilla Firefox and Opera, which hold similar, smaller shares. Safari and Chrome account for the vast majority of use on phones since they are built into iOS and Android operating systems.

Chrome Future

Google Chrome market share has increased every year from 2008 too 2025. The current trends suggest Google Chrome will likely maintain a dominant position in the immediate future.
